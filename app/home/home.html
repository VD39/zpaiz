How to play!
  • To begin enter your name.
  • You will then have 40 seconds to unmanagle as many words as possible.
  • For example, if the word to unmangle is pizza, you will presented with a mangled word, like zpaiz.
  • You will have to then enter p-i-z-z-a in the correct order to get the maxium avaible score.
  • For each character that is deleted whilst entering the word you will lose a point.
  • Now you know how to play and what the name of the game unmagled word is. ;)

Have fun playing
